## Detailed Comparison

### Ollama

- Best for macOS users seeking a hassle-free local AI experience.  
- Minimal customization but quick setup.  
- Proprietary model support limits flexibility.  
- Closed-source, which may concern privacy advocates.

### LM Studio

- Cross-platform support with a polished GUI.  
- Supports many popular LLM formats.  
- Lacks advanced extensibility and floating button convenience.  
- Semi-open source with active development.

### GGUF Loader

- Designed around the new **GGUF** model format for efficiency and standardization.  
- Unique floating button that appears on any text selection, enabling instant AI interaction anywhere on your desktop.  
- Powerful addon system for deep customization and community contributions.  
- Fully open source and privacy-focused.  
- Cross-platform with GPU acceleration and easy model loading via folder selection.

---

## Why Choose GGUF Loader?
